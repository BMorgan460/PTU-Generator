Ability: Regal Challenge Scene – Swift Action Target: Pokémon or Trainer within 5 Meters Effect:  Ask the target if they will show deference or defiance. If they choose Deference, the target loses their next Shift action and loses 3 CS in the Stat of the user’s choice.  If they choose Defiance, the user gains a +10 Bonus to all Damage Rolls for the rest of the Scene.
