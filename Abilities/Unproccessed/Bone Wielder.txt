Ability: Bone Wielder Static Effect: This ability is only functional if the user is holding a Thick Club item. The user gains a +1 Accuracy Bonus to Bone Club, Bonemerang, and Bone Rush.  Additionally, the user cannot be disarmed, or have their Thick Club forcefully removed by Trick, Switcheroo, Thief, or any other Moves or effects unless the user wishes it.
