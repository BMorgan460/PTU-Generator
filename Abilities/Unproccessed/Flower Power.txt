Ability: Flower Power At-Will – Extended Action Target: A Grass-Type Pokémon Effect: The target gains Flower Power for the rest of the day. While the target has Flower Power, they gain a +1 bonus to Skill Checks. A Pokémon with Flower Power may choose to expend their Flower Power when making an Accuracy Roll with a Grass-Type Move or when making a Save Check to gain a +2 Bonus to that roll. This Ability may target a specific target only once per day.
