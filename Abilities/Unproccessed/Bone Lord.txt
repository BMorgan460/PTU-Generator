Ability: Bone Lord At-Will - Free Action Trigger: You hit with Bone Club, Bonemerang, or Bone Rush Effect: Connection – Bonemerang. Once per Scene per Move, the following Moves may be used as if they had the following bonuses: - Bone Club: Bone Club’s target loses 1 Defense and Special Attack CS. - Bonemerang: Has a Range of Line 6 but loses the Double Strike keyword - Bone Rush: Bone Rush automatically ‘hits’ four times (as per the Five Strike keyword).
