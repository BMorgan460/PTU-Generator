Ability: Shadow Tag Scene – Free Action Target: An adjacent Trainer or Pokémon Effect: The target’s shadow becomes pinned to the target’s current spot for 5 turns. During this time, the target is Slowed and Trapped, and cannot move more than 5 meters from the spot their shadow is pinned to; even being Pushed and other forced movement effects cannot force the target to Move more than 5 meters from that spot.
