Ability: Schooling Daily - Free Action Effect: The user changes to Schooling Forme, and gains Temporary Hit Points equal to half of its own maximum Hit Points. The user cannot gain Temporary Hit Points from other sources while in Schooling Forme. When the user is below half Maximum HP and has no Temporary Hit Points left, they change back into Solo Forme. Bonus: The user has two sets of base stats; Solo and Schooling. The HP of both forms must be the same.
