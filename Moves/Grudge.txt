Move: Grudge Type: Ghost Frequency: Daily AC: None Class: Status Range: 6, 1 Target, Interrupt Effect: You may use Grudge as an Interrupt when a Damaging Attack causes the user to faint. Grudge is activated as a Free Action (does not take up a Command). The attack is resolved as usual, and the user Faints. The attacker that caused the user to Faint becomes Suppressed for the remainder of the encounter; switching and Taking a Breather does not end Suppression when used this way.
