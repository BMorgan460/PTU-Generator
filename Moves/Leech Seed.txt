Move: Leech Seed Type: Grass Frequency: Daily x2 AC: 4 Class: Status Range: 6, 1 Target Effect: At the beginning of each of the target’s turns, Leech Seed’s target loses a Tick of Hit Points. Leech Seed’s user then gains Hit Points equal to the amount the target lost. Leech Seed lasts until the target faints or is returned to a Poké Ball. Grass Types and targets immune to Grass Attacks are immune to Leech Seed. Contest Type: Smart Contest Effect: Safe Option
